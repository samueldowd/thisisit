Getting to Know Cloud Storage and Collaboration Services
By: Sam Dowd

1. Introduction
a. The students have been selected by an Executive Team Streeing Committee to
form a team of piloters to find the best cloud storage and collaboration service.
b. HBS is thinking about deploying a standard cloud storage and collaboration
service. But, they are not sure which one to choose.
c. They have narrowed it to three services
i. Microsoft SharePoint ii. Box iii. Dropbox
d. This team of students is tasked with researching and reporting on the best option.
2. The Task
a. Research the three cloud storage and collaboration services. b. Exclusively use your assigned service to create an advertisement from scratch. c. Present your advertisement to the Steering Committee
3. The Process
a. Phase I: Research
i. Visit the Phase I resources for each product and study their individual
features as well as drawbacks
ii. Complete a SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and
Threats) for each of the three services
b. Phase II: Create an Advertisement for Your Assigned Service
i. Sign up or acquire an account for the service you have been assigned ii. Work with your group members exclusively using your service to design
an advertisement using any kind of media
iii. Develop a ten minute presentation (also exclusively using your service)
that includes your advertisement and the reasons why HBS should go with your assigned service
c. Phase III: Present to the Steering Committee
4. Evaluation
a. The items you will be evaluated on are as follows (there will be a rubric for each of
these):
i. Your 3 SWOT analyses ii. Your advertisement iii. Your presentation
b. The evaluation for items II and III will have a teamwork element to them to evaluate
your ability to work with your teammates
5. Conclusion
a. Expression of gratitude from the Steering Committee b. Recap of steps taken and learnings they should take from the assignment.
