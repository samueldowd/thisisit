Discussion
The data revealed by the study shows that presentation medium, at least as far as text and
video are concerned, does not seem to matter much. The data do, however, reveal a potential disparity with the difference of five wrong answers. Further research could be done using content that is more similar to an actual lecture. For example, research could be done using longer content.
