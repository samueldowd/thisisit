Perhaps the most impressive thing about this device is its connectivity
capabilities. This device can be connected to just about any electronic you can think of and allow a person with vision impairments to use it. In addition, the device has Bluetooth capabilities allowing it to connect to wireless listening devices or even the user’s smartphone or tablet.
