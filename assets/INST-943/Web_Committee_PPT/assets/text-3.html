Why is this necessary?
1.Parents have reported that it is often difficult to find
communications from parents. Some have Moodle, some have a Google Site, some use email etc. Our goal is to make sure that parents have one standard place to look for information from any teacher.
2.The district has redesigned its web page and as part of this,
each teacher will have a web site to maintain.
3.In 2013 there will be new teacher evaluation standards
which will include using technology to communicate with parents in the category of "Family & Community Engagement".
