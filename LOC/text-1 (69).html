6. How the progra
m works:

Students enroll in their teacher's class and can create comics either independently or for an
assignment set up by their teacher. They select the layout of their comic, and then begin to add
people, backgrounds, speech bubbles, and other items. Ev
erything that is added can be
manipulated in almost endless ways. Any characters added can be modified to be in any
position, have any facial expression etc. The program even allows you to import your own
images or backgrounds for your comic book. The teac
her is able to monitor the progress of
each of their students' comic book as well.
