This product is a seven-compartment medicine tray. It is seven because it has one slot for each day of the week. At the beginning of the week the patient can fill up each slot with
the medicine for that
day and then have it all ready to take when that day comes. It is also great as a tool to remember whether the medicine was taken or not. This particular pill
reminder has two special features. It has labels on each slot for the day of the week. That way it is easy to organize and break out dosages by day. Also, it has a locking feature so that the patient can prevent anyone who shouldn’t be getting into the pills from getting to them. At the same time a caretaker could lock the pill reminder to prevent a patient from getting to the pills if there were issues with that. Lastly, because of the low cost of the pill reminder a patient could get one for each day’s dose. For example, one tray for morning medicine, and one for night medicine. The product even comes in several colors to allow the patient to color code the pill reminders.
