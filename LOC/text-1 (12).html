What I found most interesting was the classifications of disorders on the American
Speech­Language­Hearing site. I knew there were several speech disorders, but I found it interesting that they broke it down by fluency disorders and voice disorders. It was very helpful to read about the difference between the two. I also enjoyed reading the rest of the page about the language disorders and the hearing disorders. I would find it very difficult to identify the difference between any of these disorders. If anything this really increases my admiration for speech pathologists and the knowledge that they are required to have, in addition to a well­tuned and ­trained ear.
