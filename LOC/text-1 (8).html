Sam Dowd INST­968 July 11, 2013 Reflection Assignment
The assignment that I chose to reflect on is the Assistive Technology Research Paper
that we were assigned as part of Unit 3 in the INST­968 course at Framingham State University. For this assignment we were given the opportunity to choose between summarizing an article about assistive technology, or writing a paper about an emerging technology in the assistive technology space. I decided that I wanted to do something about assistive technology in the workplace, so I chose the research paper. As part of the assignment we were asked to read a journal research article about assistive technology. Then we were asked to summarize the research hypotheses, methods, and outcomes in a short paper. At this point in the course we had a solid foundation of what assistive technology is, how it is used, and even experience researching specific technologies that help people with disabilities.
The major achievement of this paper was to get me to think about assistive technology
not just in an school education setting, but in a professional setting which is much more relevant to me. I was really glad to find that much of what we had learned in the class about how assistive technologies can help in education, applied just as well to the corporate world. I struggled throughout the course to make connections to my own experience. However, after reading the research article and writing about it, I had a much better understanding of assistive technology’s role in the corporate world.
