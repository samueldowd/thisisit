There are, however, drawbacks to technology in distance education. For example,

content and assignments is needed for the online course to become more ‘learner friendly’” (p.
19). Such “retooling” is not only necessary, but led to distinct and noticeable improvement in
student performance. For an instructor

capable of taking advantage of the tools available to retool a course, it is possible that he/she
could do detriment to student learning in his/her course.
