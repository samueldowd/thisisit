Geographic Perspectives of the Environment

GEOG-812

Professor Osama Abdelgadir

Spring 2012

Reflection

In this course, I spent a lot of time learning about how geography impacts the environment. In particular, as the paper below demonstrates, I learned about the importance of biodiversity. The concept of biodiversity was an overarching theme in the course. The paper specifically discusses the effect of human beings on the natural biodiversity levels.

While the course is not directly related to my concentration, it was interesting to me as sustainability is a popular subject these days. In my work I have found it useful to use the perspectives learned in this course to help create sustainability initiatives.
