One of the major problems facing technology in education is that there aren’t enough
teachers who know how to use technology effectively. Not every school has a dedicated person to teach the teachers. So it’s up to teachers to learn on their own time and often that’s just not practical. Professional Development time is usually spent learning about other things like state standards, differentiated instruction, or the latest teaching philosophy fad. Teachers simply don’t have the opportunity to learn these skills, and so it will truly be up to the next generation of teachers to come into education knowing these skills and adopt the latest trends and use technology the way it is supposed to be used. Education is definitely at a tipping point when it comes to technology. It’s time for teachers to join the students and become good digital citizens.
