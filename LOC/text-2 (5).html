These images are transmitted via wireless or wired internet to Senticare Advisors who analyze the images to make sure that the right medicine is in the tray and then to make sure that the right medicine was taken at the right time.
This product is geared toward people who set up their medicine themselves. It not only ensure adherence to the medicine scheduled that is set up, but ensures its accuracy as well. The tray sits in the imaging dock which will also light up the appropriate slot in the tray for the medicine that needs to be taken. For example, at a
specific time on Wednesday morning, the device will light up the square where the Wednesday morning medicine is located. This helps the patient to identify what medicine to take and when. If the patient misses a dose or there is something wrong with the pill setup, the “Care Glow” will change and indicate the problem.
The device has a convenient call button on it to call the SentiCare Advisors any
time and very easily. The SentiCare advisor can help when a new prescription is written and needs to be added, when the patient is going away for a while and will need to take the tray with them, or simply when the patient needs help with something. This is a really great addition to the product as it makes it very user friendly.
