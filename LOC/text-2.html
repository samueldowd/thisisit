The WYNN Literacy Software is very powerful text-to-speech reader. It prides itself on a “bimodal” approach. It incorporates highlighting of the text being read so that the student can follow along. The product assists students with a vision
impairment by reading the text on a page to them. It also helps students with ADD or ADHD by highlighting the text that is being read so that the student can focus on that one area instead of letting their mind wander. In addition, several other features address other areas like the ability to create
notecards and other writing templates in order to enhance comprehension. The software can also be paired with a PEARL Camera so that the student can place a book under the camera and have it instantly read back to him/her. One feature of the software is that it will maintain the look and feel of the text. Instead of converting the text to just a single block of words, it will maintain the paragraph structure, the columns on a page, and even images so that the student is able to see exactly what is being read in the same format as if they were to be reading it on paper. Another feature is the ability for teachers to lockout certain tools like the dictionary, thesaurus, and spell-checking. This way, if the student is taking a test, he/she doesn’t have unauthorized access to these tools.
