Mini Lesson with Interactive PowerPoint


Title: Patterns Using an Interactive PowerPoint

Topic Covered: Patterns


This lesson will be used as a homework assignment over a few nights to give

students the opportunity to practice what they have learned about patterns.


Objectives:

Students will practice:

•

The ability to detect and repeat patterns

•

The ability to create new patterns

•

The ability to describe patterns in plain English
